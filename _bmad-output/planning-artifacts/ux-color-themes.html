What are the termination conditions and notice period?
The contract specifies two termination scenarios:

1. Termination for Convenience: Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice. All outstanding invoices must be settled within 15 business days of termination.

2. Termination for Cause: Immediate termination is permitted upon material breach, with a 10-day cure period for the breaching party.
